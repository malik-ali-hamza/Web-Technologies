Experiment 6: Web Responsiveness

Objective: Demonstrate Adaptive Layout

Resize your browser window (or view on a mobile device) to see the layout change. The **Media Query** targets screens that are 600 pixels wide or less.

Desktop View

I am wide. I use a fixed percentage width and float next to my neighbor.

Mobile View

When the screen shrinks, I stack below my neighbor and take up full width.

Report Requirements and Instructions

Experiment 6 requires the demonstration of screen size adaptability:

  1. The **viewport** meta tag must be included in the head section.
  2. **Internal CSS** is used.
  3. A **Media Query** (using `@media only screen and (max-width: Xpx)`) must be defined.
  4. The layout and/or styling must change when viewed on a mobile-sized screen.
  5. Include screenshots of **Desktop View** and **Mobile View** in your report.
